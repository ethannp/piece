Why is there a timer?

1. I liked the idea that you had pressure to submit a drawing, so you were more focused on getting the general idea of it rather than all the specifics.
2. Firebase has limits on how large values can be (the data of each piece). To avoid any chance of hitting this limit, I have a cap of 150 seconds, plus the string compression, so even constant drawing should not hit the limit (as drawings are saved by paths, not pixels).
